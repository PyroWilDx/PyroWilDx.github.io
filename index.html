About Me

Hi! I'm Pierre, a French student at Grenoble INP – Ensimag. My journey with Computer Science began in high school, where I wrote my first line of code. From that moment, I found my passion and decided to pursue my studies in this field, exposing myself to diverse topics.

Outside the limits of my studies, my passion led me to create various personal projects. These projects allowed me to acquire a wide range of skills, beyond what I could have learned through my academic studies alone.

My focus lies in Software Development (with a special affinity for Game Development) using programming languages such as C, C++, or Java. I like to write Qualitative and Efficient code. Through my projects, I had the opportunity to learn using all kinds of tools, including Unreal, Godot, or SDL2. Meanwhile, my studies exposed me to various topics in Maths and Computer Science.
